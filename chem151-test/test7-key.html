21. A car is traveling at 60.0 miles per hour. What is this speed in meters per second (m/s)? (1 mile = 1609 m)
Answer: 26.8 m/s
(60.0 mi/hr) * (1609 m/mi) * (1 hr/3600 s) = 26.816... m/s.
22. A compound is composed of 52.2% Carbon, 13.1% Hydrogen, and 34.7% Oxygen by mass. What is its empirical formula?
Answer: C2H6O
C: 52.2/12.01 = 4.35. H: 13.1/1.01 = 13.0. O: 34.7/16.00 = 2.17.
Divide by 2.17: C=2, H=6, O=1.
23. Calculate the mass of CO2 produced from the complete combustion of 5.00 g of ethane (C2H6).
2 C2H6 + 7 O2 → 4 CO2 + 6 H2O
Answer: 14.6 g
Moles C2H6 = 5.00 g / 30.07 g/mol = 0.166 mol.
Moles CO2 = 0.166 mol * (4/2) = 0.332 mol.
Mass CO2 = 0.332 mol * 44.01 g/mol = 14.6 g.
24. If the theoretical yield of a reaction is 25.0 g and the actual yield is 20.0 g, what is the percent yield?
Answer: 80.0%
(20.0 g / 25.0 g) * 100 = 80.0%.
25. Consider the reaction 2 H2 + O2 → 2 H2O. If you start with 4.0 moles of H2 and 3.0 moles of O2, how many moles of H2O can be produced?
Answer: 4.0 moles
4.0 mol H2 needs 2.0 mol O2. We have 3.0 mol O2 (Excess).
H2 is limiting. 4.0 mol H2 produces 4.0 mol H2O.
