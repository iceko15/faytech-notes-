21. Convert 90.0 km/hr to m/s. (1 km = 1000 m, 1 hr = 3600 s)
Answer: 25.0 m/s
(90.0 km/hr) * (1000 m/km) * (1 hr/3600 s) = 25.0 m/s.
22. A compound is 85.7% Carbon and 14.3% Hydrogen by mass. What is its empirical formula?
Answer: CH2
C: 85.7/12.01=7.14. H: 14.3/1.01=14.16.
Ratio H/C = 2. Formula is CH2.
23. Calculate the mass of MgO produced from the reaction of 12.15 g of Mg with excess O2.
2 Mg + O2 → 2 MgO
Answer: 20.15 g
Moles Mg = 12.15 g / 24.31 g/mol = 0.500 mol.
Moles MgO = 0.500 mol.
Mass MgO = 0.500 mol * 40.31 g/mol = 20.155 g.
24. If the theoretical yield is 50.0 g and the actual yield is 42.0 g, what is the percent yield?
Answer: 84.0%
(42.0 / 50.0) * 100 = 84.0%.
25. For the reaction 2 H2 + O2 → 2 H2O, if you have 10.0 moles of H2 and 10.0 moles of O2, which is the limiting reactant?
Answer: H2
10.0 mol H2 needs 5.0 mol O2. We have 10.0 mol O2 (Excess). H2 is limiting.
