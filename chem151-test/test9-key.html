21. A car travels at 60.0 miles per hour. Convert this speed to meters per second (m/s). (1 mile = 1609 m)
Answer: 26.8 m/s
(60.0 mi/hr) * (1609 m/mi) * (1 hr/3600 s) = 26.8 m/s.
22. A compound is 70.0% Iron and 30.0% Oxygen by mass. What is its empirical formula?
Answer: Fe2O3
Fe: 70.0/55.85=1.25. O: 30.0/16.00=1.875.
Ratio O/Fe = 1.5. Multiply by 2: Fe2O3.
23. Calculate the mass of CO2 produced from the combustion of 16.0 g of methane (CH4).
CH4 + 2 O2 → CO2 + 2 H2O
Answer: 44.0 g
Moles CH4 = 16.0 g / 16.04 g/mol ≈ 1.00 mol.
Moles CO2 = 1.00 mol.
Mass CO2 = 1.00 mol * 44.01 g/mol = 44.0 g.
24. If the theoretical yield is 50.0 g and the actual yield is 45.0 g, what is the percent yield?
Answer: 90.0%
(45.0 / 50.0) * 100 = 90.0%.
25. For the reaction 2 H2 + O2 → 2 H2O, if you start with 4.0 moles of H2 and 1.0 mole of O2, which is the limiting reactant?
Answer: O2
1.0 mol O2 needs 2.0 mol H2. We have 4.0 mol H2 (Excess). O2 is limiting.
