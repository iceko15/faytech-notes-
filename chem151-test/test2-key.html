21. Convert a speed of 65.0 miles per hour to meters per second. (1 mile = 1609 m, 1 hour = 3600 s)
Answer: 29.1 m/s
(65.0 mi/hr) * (1609 m/mi) * (1 hr/3600 s) = 29.05... m/s. Rounded to 3 sig figs.
22. A compound is found to be 75.0% carbon and 25.0% hydrogen by mass. What is its empirical formula?
Answer: CH4
C: 75.0g / 12.01 = 6.24 mol. H: 25.0g / 1.01 = 24.75 mol.
Ratio H/C = 24.75 / 6.24 ≈ 4.
23. Given the reaction: 2 Na + Cl2 → 2 NaCl. How many grams of NaCl can be produced from 10.0 g of Na?
Answer: 25.4 g
10.0 g Na * (1 mol Na / 22.99 g) * (2 mol NaCl / 2 mol Na) * (58.44 g NaCl / 1 mol NaCl) = 25.4 g NaCl.
24. In a reaction, the theoretical yield of a product is 20.0 g. A student carries out the experiment and obtains 15.0 g of the product. What is the percent yield?
Answer: 75.0%
(Actual / Theoretical) * 100 = (15.0 g / 20.0 g) * 100 = 75.0%.
25. For the reaction N2 + 3H2 → 2NH3, if you start with 10.0 g of N2 and 10.0 g of H2, which is the limiting reactant?
Answer: N2
Moles N2 = 10.0g / 28.02 = 0.357 mol. Moles H2 = 10.0g / 2.02 = 4.95 mol.
0.357 mol N2 needs 0.357 * 3 = 1.071 mol H2. You have more than enough H2, so N2 is limiting.
