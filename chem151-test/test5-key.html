21. An object has a mass of 50.0 g and a density of 2.50 g/mL. What is its volume?
Answer: 20.0 mL
Volume = Mass / Density = 50.0 g / 2.50 g/mL = 20.0 mL.
22. How many grams of NaOH are in 500. mL of a 0.200 M solution?
Answer: 4.00 g
Moles = 0.200 M * 0.500 L = 0.100 mol.
Grams = 0.100 mol * 40.00 g/mol = 4.00 g.
23. For the reaction 4Al + 3O2 → 2Al2O3, how many moles of Al2O3 can be produced from 10.0 moles of Al?
Answer: 5.00 mol
10.0 mol Al * (2 mol Al2O3 / 4 mol Al) = 5.00 mol Al2O3.
24. A compound is 40.9% C, 4.58% H, and 54.5% O by mass. What is its empirical formula?
Answer: C3H4O3
C: 40.9/12.01=3.41. H: 4.58/1.01=4.53. O: 54.5/16=3.41.
Divide by 3.41: C=1, O=1, H=1.33. Multiply by 3 to get whole numbers: C3H4O3.
25. If the theoretical yield of a reaction is 50.0 g and the percent yield is 90.0%, what was the actual yield?
Answer: 45.0 g
Actual Yield = Theoretical Yield * (Percent Yield / 100)
Actual Yield = 50.0 g * (90.0 / 100) = 45.0 g.
