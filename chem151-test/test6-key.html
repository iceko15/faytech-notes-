21. A runner completes a 10.0 km race in 45.0 minutes. What is their average speed in meters per second (m/s)?
Answer: 3.70 m/s
Distance = 10.0 km * 1000 m/km = 10,000 m.
Time = 45.0 min * 60 s/min = 2700 s.
Speed = 10,000 m / 2700 s = 3.7037... m/s → 3.70 m/s.
22. What is the empirical formula of a compound that is 30.4% Nitrogen and 69.6% Oxygen by mass?
Answer: NO2
N: 30.4 g / 14.01 g/mol = 2.17 mol.
O: 69.6 g / 16.00 g/mol = 4.35 mol.
Ratio O/N = 4.35 / 2.17 ≈ 2. Formula is NO2.
23. Propane (C3H8) burns in oxygen to produce carbon dioxide and water. How many grams of CO2 are produced from the combustion of 11.0 g of propane?
C3H8 + 5 O2 → 3 CO2 + 4 H2O
Answer: 33.0 g
Moles C3H8 = 11.0 g / 44.1 g/mol = 0.249 mol.
Moles CO2 = 0.249 mol * 3 = 0.748 mol.
Mass CO2 = 0.748 mol * 44.01 g/mol = 32.9 g (approx 33.0 g).
24. In a reaction, 4.00 g of H2 reacts with excess O2 to form water. If the theoretical yield is 36.0 g and the actual yield is 30.0 g, what is the percent yield?
Answer: 83.3%
Percent Yield = (Actual / Theoretical) * 100
(30.0 g / 36.0 g) * 100 = 83.3%.
25. For the reaction N2 + 3 H2 → 2 NH3, if you start with 2.0 moles of N2 and 5.0 moles of H2, how many moles of NH3 can be produced?
Answer: 3.3 moles
Check limiting: 2.0 mol N2 needs 6.0 mol H2. We have 5.0 mol H2 (Limiting).
Moles NH3 = 5.0 mol H2 * (2 mol NH3 / 3 mol H2) = 3.33 mol.
